My Neighbor Totoro Bento Box

Description

Satsukiâ€™s bento from Totoro is a perfect example of a cute and healthy typical bento. A lot of these ingredients are common in Japan.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • 1 umeboshi
  • 1 shishamo
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • handful frozen edamame
  • 1 tbsp sakura denbu

Instructions

  1. Put cooked rice in your bento box about 3/4 of the way full.
  2. Take 1 umeboshi and put it on one side of the rice.
  3. In a small pan, heat the oil on medium-high.
  4. Cook one shishamo on both sides until cooked.
  5. Place the cooked shishamo across the middle of the bento box on the rice.
  6. Boil water in a small pot.
  7. Put in a handful of frozen edamame and cook according to package instructions.
  8. Once cooked, pop the edamame beans out of the pod.
  9. Arrange them on the rice to fill up a little less than one quarter of the bento box, under the shishamo.
  10. Sprinkle the sakura denbu on the rice next to the edamame.
  11. Enjoy!
