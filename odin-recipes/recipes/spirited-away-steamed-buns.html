Spirited Away Steamed Buns

Description

Contrary to belief, it's actually quite easy to make a red bean paste at home, and the same could be said for the bao dough. The only way we can make a fluffy, pillowy bun stuffed with sweet red bean filling better? Make it huge of course!

Ingredients

  • 200g red bean paste filling
  • 300g all purpose flour
  • 40g sugar
  • 1.5 tsp sugar tolerant instant yeast
  • 150ml warm water
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • a tiny pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a stand mixer, place all of the dough ingredients in and then knead for 6-8 minutes at slow speed.
  2. Shape the dough into a ball and set aside in warm place for 1 hour or until double in size.
  3. Add around 2 tablespoon of flour in the dough and continue kneading the dough for 2 minutes at low speed in a stand mixer.
  4. Then transfer out and divide the dough into 6 or 8 portions.
  5. Shape each portion to a round ball.
  6. Press it to a wrapper with thin edges and thicker center.
  7. Place around 1.5 tablespoon of red bean fillings in center.
  8. Seal completely and turn over and shape it into a dome.
  9. Place the buns on baking papers and then place in steamer.
  10. Cover the lid and rest for another 15 to 20 minutes at a room temperature around 28 degree C to 30 degree C.
  11. In cold water days, heat water in a pot for several minutes until warm but not boiling and then place the steamer on the warm water, rest for 15 to 20 minutes.
  12. Start the fire and steam the buns for another 20 minutes, turn off fire and stand for 5 minutes before enjoying.
  13. Re-steam the buns if they are cooled.
  14. Enjoy!
